## Hobbies & Things That Make Me Happy  

### **Coding things nobody asked for**  
I like programming random things just to try them out. Since I’m still in my first year, there are a lot of concepts I don’t fully understand yet, so I experiment out of curiosity. It’s my way of learning — breaking things, fixing them, and figuring out how everything works.

### **Drawing when inspiration hits**  
I’ve always liked drawing. I don’t do it every day, but when that spark hits, I grab my tablet or a pencil and just let it flow. It’s something that’s been with me since I was a kid.

### **Working out so I don’t become an NPC**  
Exercising has been part of my routine for about three years now. Some months I go more, some months less — exams, trips, life — but I never drop it completely. It keeps me active, clears my mind, and makes me feel like I’m not just sitting around like a background character.

### **Listening to music and reading anything**  
This is my comfort zone. Music + reading is how I relax, disconnect, and reset. It’s the same vibe I get from watching anime — peaceful, familiar, and grounding.

### **Baking cakes or trying new recipes**  
I love sweets, so baking is something I genuinely enjoy. Cakes, pastries, anything related to desserts — it’s fun, relaxing, and the results are always worth it. I also like trying new recipes just to see how they turn out.
